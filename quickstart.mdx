## Get Started

Follow these steps to begin using Moony for P2P payments.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Flipcash">
    Download the Flipcash mobile app to access Moony. Flipcash provides the first public interface for interacting with the Moony Reserve and sending P2P payments.

    <Warning>
    Flipcash is available for iOS and Android. Make sure you download from the official app store. <a href="https://www.flipcash.com/download" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Download Flipcash</a>
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Moony">
    Once you have Flipcash installed, you can access Moony directly through the app. Moony is available as MNY (the native digital asset) and integrates seamlessly with Flipcash's payment interface.

    <Check>
    Flipcash provides immediate access to Moony through their mobile payment platform. <a href="#">Access Moony</a>
    </Check>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Acquire MNY">
    Buy MNY through the Moony Reserve using USDF. Through Flipcash's intuitive interface, you can seamlessly convert between Moony and USDF to acquire the amount you need.

    <Info>
    The Moony Reserve uses a deterministic bonding curve to determine pricing. Each transaction updates pricing automatically according to the protocol's immutable rules.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send P2P Payments">
    Start sending peer-to-peer payments instantly. Flipcash offers zero-fee P2P transfers, allowing you to send MNY directly to others or share payment links. All transactions are trustless and settle onchain with finality.

    <Check>
    You're now ready to use Moony for P2P payments!
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
