## About Moony Labs

<div style={{ textAlign: 'justify' }}>

Moony was deployed by Moony Labs, LLC. as a decentralized monetary protocol designed as a digital public good. The protocol uses open-source infrastructure and operates autonomously through an immutable smart contract, ensuring that no single entity maintains control over its operation or distribution mechanisms.
