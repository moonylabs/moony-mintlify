## Transaction Properties

<p style={{ textAlign: 'justify', marginBottom: '2rem' }}>
Transactions with MNY do not require approval from centralized authorities and cannot be selectively blocked or filtered by third parties. Transfers are executed onchain and, once confirmed, are irreversible. MNY is designed to support direct ownership and voluntary self-custody. Participants may hold and transfer the asset without surrendering control to intermediaries, allowing value to move as freely as information across the network. These properties enable censorship-resistant exchange and open participation in economic activity, extending access to anyone, anywhere, on equal terms.
</p>

<p style={{ textAlign: 'justify', marginBottom: '3rem' }}>
Moony has a fixed maximum supply of 21 million units, an immutable constraint that cryptographically ensures the protocol's long-term economic integrity. There is no mechanism by which additional units can ever be created, guaranteeing that the total supply remains permanently capped. This fixed supply protects Moony from monetary debasement, distinguishing it from traditional fiat currencies whose issuance can be expanded through discretionary policy, often resulting in the long-term erosion of purchasing power. By removing the possibility of discretionary supply expansion, the Moony protocol establishes a stable foundation for decentralized economic coordination. Scarcity is enforced by code rather than trust, enabling participation on predictable and equal terms over time.
</p>
