<Info>

Peer-to-peer (P2P) payments represent Moony's most direct utility: enabling value transfer between individuals without intermediaries. By combining a credibly fixed supply with onchain settlement, Moony delivers global digital cash with finality, transparency, and negligible fees.

</Info>

<div style={{ textAlign: 'justify' }}>

Legacy payment systems rely on layered intermediaries such as banks, processors, or card networks, each introducing cost, latency, and potential for control or exclusion. Transactions can be delayed, reversed, or denied entirely, with entire regions excluded from participation.

## Financial Inclusion

<div style={{ backgroundColor: '#E7D9FD', borderRadius: '1rem', padding: '0', overflow: 'hidden', marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}>

</div>

<div style={{ textAlign: 'justify' }}>

Billions of people remain underserved, or entirely excluded, by traditional financial systems. In many regions, the absence of reliable banking infrastructure leaves individuals unable to store value securely in sound currency, send money across borders, or participate in even the most basic forms of commerce. High inflation and economic instability further erode purchasing power, making it difficult to preserve wealth or plan for the future.

</div>

<div style={{ textAlign: 'justify', marginTop: '1rem' }}>

Where banking does exist, high fees, slow settlement times, and rigid requirements place meaningful participation out of reach for many. Cross‑border remittances can take days to arrive, cost double‑digit percentages in fees, and are often impractical for small amounts, limiting economic mobility and reinforcing systemic barriers.

</div>

<div style={{ textAlign: 'justify', marginTop: '1rem' }}>

Moony's peer‑to‑peer architecture offers a different path: one that bypasses the bottlenecks of legacy finance and extends open participation to anyone with a mobile device. By removing intermediaries and leveraging low‑cost, high‑speed blockchain settlement, Moony makes direct digital transactions viable at a scale and inclusivity level traditional rails cannot match.

</div>

## Interoperable Access

<div style={{ textAlign: 'justify' }}>

Moony was minted as an SPL token on the Solana blockchain, ensuring it is fully interoperable within the broader Solana blockchain ecosystem, not bound to any single interface or platform. From the moment Moony is acquired through the Reserve, it is part of a broader composable network where users and developers can freely move, store, and integrate the asset without permission.

</div>

<div style={{ textAlign: 'justify', marginTop: '1rem' }}>

This open design not only reinforces Moony's role as decentralized digital cash but also guarantees that its utility can expand organically across a variety of independent applications.
